This blog discusses how the Amazon Web Services (AWS) NBA Draft Combine gives teams one of their first concentrated looks at the next generation of NBA talent. Over several days, prospects undergo a wide range of basketball activities and evaluation. The results can help explain why a player is rising or how a particular skill may translate to the court.
